Steak Alfredo Recipe

Descripton


This is a take on a classic dish, this steak alfredo will blow your mind. It is prepared with the upmost care and consideration for the meat, which is a main staple in this dish. Let your taste buds melt to it's elegant flavor.


Alfredo Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups of 2% milk
  • 1 1/2 cups of heavy cream
  • 1/2 cup of parmasean cheese
  • 1/2 cup of Romano cheese
  • 6 jumbo egg yolks
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste

Marinade Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups italian-style salad dressing
  • 1 tablespoon fresh rosemary
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 2 poinds flat iron steak, cut into 3 inch squares
  • 4 cups chopped fresh spinach
  • 4 tablespoons crumbled Gorgonzola cheese, divided
  • 1 pound dry fettuccine pasta
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic glaze
  • chopped fresh parsley, or as needed

Prepartion and Steps

  1. Heat milk and saucepan over medium heat until simmer.
  2. Slowly whip in Parmesan and Romano cheese then remove from heat.
  3. Whisk egg yolks together in a heat-proof bowl then slowly add 1/4 cup of hot sauce.
  4. Whisk egg mixture into saucepan slowly, season with salt and pepper.
  5. Allow alfredo sauce to cool and refrigerate until needed.
  6. Combine salad dressing, rosemary, and lemon juice to a large bowl.
  7. Add steak, stir, and coat the mixture.
  8. Marinate in refrigerator for 4 hours.

  9. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium heat and lightly oil the grate.
  10. Fill a large pot with lightly salted water and bring to a boil.
  11. Cook fettucine at boil until tender, about 8 minutes.
  12. Heat 2 cups of refrigerated alfredo sauce over medium heat.
  13. Add spinach and 2 tablespoons of Gorgonzola cheese.
  14. Remove steak from marinade and cook for 5 to 10 minutes.
  15. Pour hot alfredo sauce into a large serving bowl, add pasta, and stir until combined.
  16. Add grilled steak and drizzle the meat with balsamic glaze. Garnish with parsley.
