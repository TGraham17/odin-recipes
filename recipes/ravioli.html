Balsamic Ravioli

Description

This is a robust flavored ravioli that will melt in your mouth. It has subtle but rich flavors of ricotta cheese, parmesan, with a kick of balsamic.

Ingredients

  • 1/2 cup walnuts
  • 1(25 ounce) package of store-bought ravioli
  • 2 tablespoons butter
  • 2 tablespoons balsamic vinegar
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Preparation and Steps

  1. Toast walnuts in skillet over medium heat for 8 to 10 minutes. Remove from skillet.
  2. Fill pot with lightly salted water bring to boil.
  3. Stir in ravioli and return to boil. Cook until ravioli floats about 3 to 5 minutes.
  4. Warm butter in a skillet over medium heat until slightly brown.
  5. Add balsamic vinegar, stir until combined for 1 to 2 minutes.
  6. Stir in ravioli, mix until combined. Top with walnuts and Parmesan cheese.
